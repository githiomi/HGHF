WHO WE ARE

Reality Outreach Ministries international is the franchise owner of the famous, unique and powerful outreach tool of our time renowned as “Heaven’s Gates & Hell’s Flames ©” Drama with one common goal - impact the local community one production at a time. Heaven’s Gates and Hells flames drama has been in operation for forty four years.

Heaven’s Gates drama is a powerful, dramatic and cinematic presentation of the Gospel message, combining ageless, biblical truth with modern sound, lighting and multimedia effects in addition to an incredible stage set. This drama present a professional evangelistic event with overwhelming appreciation and feedback of lives being transformed
